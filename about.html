UNISEM unites scientists, engineers, and mathematicians to create a supportive community that inspires and empowers our future leaders through education, research, and public service.

A Culture of Discovery

Our partnerships with UCSD's distinguished professors have allowed UNISEM to offer all of our scientists and mathematicians a guaranteed research position at one of UCSD's premier labs. Our workshops and training curriculum go further by preparing students with the skills needed to design and execute their own research projects. This way, we create a mutually beneficial relationship between the faculty mentor and the student, providing a learning environment and support structure that prepares students for graduate and medical school.

A Launch Pad for Innovation

For our engineers, UNISEM has prepared a program that fosters innovative products and start-up culture. Unlike a hackathon, UNISEM recreates the work environment at major companies by bringing together students from a variety of disciplines, including business and design, to work on market-ready products. Throughout the year, students work on bringing these products to market and compete for prizes at our annual symposium. UNISEM's relationships with influential companies and experienced entrepreneurs then provides teams with the insight needed to convert ideas into successful start-ups.

UNISEM's Minimum Requirements

  • 3.5 Cumulative GPA for Science and Math Majors
  • 3.3 Cumulative GPA for Engineering and Computer Science Majors
  • Note: UNISEM is free for all members.

A Community of Leaders.

We believe that unity is strength, knowledge is power, and attitude is everything. Our programs bring together the best scholars from UCSD's strongest disciplines, creating a community that fosters creativity, leadership, and support. Our various community service events, our student-run tutoring center, and our individual academic and career advising ensure students reach their true potential as professionals and members of the community.
